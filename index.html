Welcome to Precise Academy

At Precise Transport and Logistics Academy, we are committed to providing world-class education and training in the field of transportation and logistics. Our programs are designed to equip students with the practical skills and theoretical knowledge needed to excel in this dynamic industry.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced instructors, and industry partnerships, we offer a comprehensive learning experience that prepares our graduates for successful careers in various sectors of transport and logistics.

Whether you're just starting your career or looking to advance in the field, Precise Academy offers the right programs to help you achieve your professional goals.

Apply for Professional Advanced Diploma Examination

The Professional Advanced Examinations course includes two parts of written examinations which covers five modules each. To sit for the examinations, you will need to have achieved or been exempted from the Foundation and Professional Diploma Stage One Examinations modules respectively. Successful completion of the Professional Advanced Diploma in Transport Management and Administration examinations qualifies you for the award of ‘‘Associate Membership’’ of the Institute, and gives you eligibility to apply for Professional Graduate Diploma Final Qualifying Exams.

Apply for Postgraduate Professional Diploma Examination

To study the Postgraduate Professional Diploma Final Qualifying Examinations stage, you will need to have achieved or been exempted from the Foundation, Professional Diploma and Professional Advanced Diploma modules respectively. The course consists of some modules to be written as exams.
